Hello, I'm Benjamin Hamlin

Education

I graduated Fife High School in 2009 where soon after I attended Bates Technical College. At Bates I studied Database Technology with a focus on Oracle databases.

After graduating from Bates, I attended Tacoma Community College to obtain a degree that would transfer to a university. I studied Java programming in TCC's Computer Science program while I finished up the majority of the general education requirements.

I graduated from Washington State University with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science in May 2019. At WSU, I focussed on programming in C and C++ even going as far as being a teaching assistant leading a lab teaching C to students in CS 121.

Work

While attending WSU, I was an undergraduate teaching assistant for Andrew O'Fallon for two semesters. As a TA, I lead a three hour lab where the students would put into practice what they had learned the week prior. Often, a summary of concepts would be given during the first half hour.

During my junior year, I was a Residential Technology Assistant (RTA) for Stephenson East Residence Hall. For one semester my TA position overlapped with my RTA job. As an RTA, I was a liason between ITS at WSU and the students of the hall I was assigned. This meant that I submitted tickets for problems with the network in the hall and solved minor problems students were experiencing. I worked with students of the hall to solve printing problems and answer any technology related questions.
